# FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

## Authentication & Account Issues

### Why can't I log in to my account?

There are several common reasons why you might be unable to log in:

1. **Incorrect credentials**: Make sure your email and password are correct. Passwords are case-sensitive.
2. **Account not activated**: Check your email for an activation link. If you didn't receive one, try requesting a new activation email.
3. **Too many failed login attempts**: Your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons. Wait 30 minutes and try again.
4. **Browser cache/cookies**: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try logging in again.
5. **Session expired**: If you were logged in previously, your session may have expired. Log out completely and log back in.

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

1. Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if you don't see it)
4. Click the link in the email
5. Enter your new password and confirm it
6. Log in with your new password

**Note**: Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If yours has expired, request a new one.

### I didn't receive the password reset email

If you don't receive the password reset email:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Make sure you entered the correct email address
3. Wait a few minutes - emails can take time to arrive
4. Try requesting another reset link
5. Contact support if you still don't receive it after 1 hour

### How do I change my password?

To change your password when you're already logged in:

1. Go to Settings (usually in the top right corner)
2. Click on "Account" or "Security"
3. Select "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password twice to confirm
6. Click "Update Password"

## Account & Profile

### How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings → Profile
3. Edit the information you want to change
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Available fields:**
- Name
- Email address
- Company
- Phone number
- Bio/Description

### Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can change your email address:

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Click on "Change Email"
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the new email (check your inbox for verification link)
5. Your email will be updated once verified

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account permanently:

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm your password
4. Review the deletion notice
5. Confirm deletion

**Warning**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data will be lost.

## Billing & Plans

### What plans are available?

We offer three plans:

- **Free**: For getting started (limited features)
- **Pro**: For professional users ($29/month)
- **Enterprise**: For large organizations (custom pricing)

[See detailed plan comparison](plans.md)

### How do I upgrade my plan?

To upgrade your plan:

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select the plan you want
4. Enter your payment information
5. Confirm the purchase

Your plan will be upgraded immediately, and you'll have access to all premium features.

### How do I downgrade my plan?

To downgrade your plan:

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Manage Plan"
3. Select the plan you want to downgrade to
4. Confirm downgrade

Your plan will be downgraded at the end of your current billing cycle.

## Support & Troubleshooting

### What should I do if I encounter a bug?

If you find a bug:

1. Try to reproduce the issue
2. Note the exact steps to reproduce it
3. Check if you're using the latest version
4. Contact support with:
   - What happened
   - What you expected to happen
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Your browser/device information

### How long does it take to get a response from support?

Our support team typically responds within:

- **Free plan**: 48-72 hours
- **Pro plan**: 24 hours
- **Enterprise plan**: 4 hours (during business hours)

### Where can I report a security issue?

For security issues, please email: security@example.com

**Do not** create a public support ticket for security issues.
